This video features Anne Baillot (Centre Marc Bloch, Berlin) talking about the
Digital Edition 'Letters and Texts: Intellectual Berlin around 1800'. This
online digital edition presents texts from 1800 to 1830 that were, until this
day, either completely unpublished or published in an abridged form. This work
is funded by the German Research Foundation.
