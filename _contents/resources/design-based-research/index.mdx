In this lecture, Tony Hall examines design-based research (DBR) in educational
contexts and settings. Drawing on key contemporary concepts and literature in
educational design research, he focuses on how design-based research can be
adapted and adopted, both to develop and deploy bespoke educational innovations
and technologies.

The talk was delivered as a keynote at the opening of the #dariahTeach Open
Humanities Workshop and hosted by the Belgrade Center for Digital Humanities at
the Faculty of Media and Communication.
